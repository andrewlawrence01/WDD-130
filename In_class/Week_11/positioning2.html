The wind begins a gentle cry,

It hums across the open plain,

It lifts the leaves into the sky,

It dances through the meadow grass,

It stirs the lake with rippling grace,

It sings of all the storms that pass.

It weaves a song through ancient trees,

It rushes down the mountain’s side,

A spirit loosed in autumn’s breeze.

Endings can come fast,

But memories stay,

So hold them to your heart until the world's last.
